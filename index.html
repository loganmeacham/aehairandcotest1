Our Mission

Our goal is a feel-good experience in a modern, laid-back atmosphere with just the right amount of luxury and small-town charm. We are grateful to serve historic downtown Ringgold and the surrounding areas with a wide range of hair services provided by highly educated stylists who truly care about you.

We take our time hiring good human beings who elevate each other and actually listen to your wants and needs. AE Hair + Co wants you to leave your experience feeling beautiful, feeling heard, and feeling like your best you. Ready to raise the standard.

- AE Hair + Co.
